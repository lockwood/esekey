Wisteria is a ground floor apartment having one bedroom with twin beds and a living/dining room.

The apartment has sofa chairs that open out to form single 3ft wide beds and can sleep a maximum of 5. There is a shower over the bath and a fully equipped kitchen comprising hob and oven, dishwasher, microwave, fridge, toaster and kettle and a television and table, chairs and sofa in the living area.
