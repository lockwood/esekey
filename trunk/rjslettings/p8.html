  Gardener's Bothy is a first floor barn conversion with two bedrooms - the second is very small.

The main bedroom has a king size bed.
The apartment has an open plan living/dining/kitchen area.

The kitchen includes a hob and oven, microwave, fridge, toaster and kettle.

There is plenty of seating and a television in the living area.
