  Directions from M4 Junction 7
  • At Junction 7 (Slough West) take the spur road to the A4
  • At the roundabout turn left toward Maidenhead
  • Go straight over the traffic lights and at the next roundabout (Sainsbury's on left) turn left following the sign to Dorney
  • Drive over the motorway past the pub on the right (The Pineapple) and through Dorney Village
  • You will also pass The Palmer Arms pub on your left
  • Soon you will come to a cattle grid entering onto Dorney Common
  • After about 300m turn right following sign to Boveney
  • Drive straight on over small humpbacked bridge towards the end of Boveney Road
  • There follows a sharp bend with bad visibility (pleas drive slowly) and the road is now sign posted Lock Path
  • Drive to the very end of the road until you are facing a cattle grid and an avenue of chestnut trees
  • The Old Place is immediately on your left behind the wrought iron gates. Please refer to gate instructions for access
