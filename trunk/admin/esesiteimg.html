Step Description
1 To replace an existing image on your site, or to add a new one, you must upload the new image from your computer to the EseSite™ server. Before you do this, you will need to ensure that the new image is of a suitable size and quality for its intended location, otherwise there is a risk that your efforts may have a detrimental effect on the page, rather than improving it. If you need help in preparing an image for use on your site, please call 07860 832741 or email support@esekey.com.
2 To upload the new image, click on "Image List" in the EseSite™ Menu on the left of the page. This page contains a list of all the images that are currently stored in your site storage on the EseSite™ server. Below this list is a text box labelled "Select Image for upload".
3 Click Browse... to open a Choose File dialog window and use it to browse your computer to find the folder that contains the image you want to upload. When you have found the image and selected it in the dialog window, click the Open button. The path and filename of the image you selected is now displayed in the text box.
4 Click on the Upload button to begin uploading the image to the server. Depending on the speed of your internet connection and the size of the image, the upload may take several moments to complete.
5 When the upload completes, you will see a pop-up message - File uploaded: - followed by the name of the image file that you selected. You must now update the site content to indicate where on your site you wish the image to be shown. If you want the image to be shown in the Gallery, follow the instructions described in the section Updating the Gallery. If you want the image to be shown on one of the other pages, follow the instructions described in the section Updating Page Content. If you wish, you can add the new image to one or more pages of the site and include it in the gallery.
