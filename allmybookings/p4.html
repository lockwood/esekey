Standard Pricing

The standard package is priced at £5.00 per bookable property per month. There are no commission charges and no set up fees.

For webmasters/webmistresses who are in a position to make this package available to a wider customer base, there is a negotiable intermediary rate so that their customers pay the standard package price and the intermediary can take a small percentage of the revenues.

Terms and Conditions

Charges are payable on a quarterly basis in advance.There is no minimum contract period - the contract can be cancelled at any time.

Preferred payment method is by quarterly standing order direct to Esekey Limited. Individuals or companies acting as intermediaries in the provision of the package to customers are limited to a maximum of 10% of package revenues from their customers.
