With an office in Shepperton, Decorite provides plastering and decorating to clients throughout the Shepperton, Staines, Sunbury and Heathrow areas. Formed in 1988 the company has many years' experience in the plastering craft.

With a staff body of 5 skilled trades people, Decorite has a professional team, qualified to city and guilds. The firm provides local people with a professional plastering and decorating service and has access to many other trades. The expert staff help clients increase the value of their home. The service is intended to be cost effective every time.

Decorite has an expanding network of clients, located in and around Shepperton, including private and commercial.
