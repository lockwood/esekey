Sheephouse Manor was originally a farmhouse, built some 500 years ago, and retains all the old world charm with original oak beams, timber floors and miniature antique pine doors.

Our accommodation is ideal for both professional working people and family holidays.

We offer short lets from 1 week to 6 months for contractors or relocations.

For family vacations we have a childrens' play area, large gardens, plus a paddock. Legoland packages can be booked with your accommodation.

There is secure off street parking within the grounds. Pets are welcome by arrangement.

We aim to provide the conveniences of a hotel but with the comforts of a home.

We have comprehensive access statements for the Self Catering Cottages and The West Wing.
