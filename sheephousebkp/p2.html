The West Wing can be booked as a self contained three bedroom self catering suite. It has its own private entrance, and fully equipped kitchen/dining room. Upstairs are 3 bedrooms; one double and two twin, sleeping a total of 6 people. Cot and high chair available.

Bed and breakfast accommodation is also housed in The West Wing of the original Manor House, and comprises The Cookham Room, with double (Queen size)bed, and  ensuite bathroom; The Windsor Room, with twin beds, and en-suite shower; and The Eton Room, a twin room with private shower room adjacent.
