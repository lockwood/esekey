Each of our four cottages, set within the delightful grounds of Sheephouse Manor, has been tastefully converted from stables and a milking parlour. The cottages, which overlook farmland and lakes, sleep between 1 - 5 people and can be booked by singles, couples or family groups. All cottages are available from 1 week to 6 month stays. We are ideal for business relocations, short stay contractors up to 6 months, and family holidays.

The cottages range in size from studio through to 2 bedrooms, and each has its own fully equipped kitchen, ensuite bathroom, dining area, TV with DVD player, and 24 hour access to laundry facilities. Patio furniture and BBQ are available, as is access to large gardens and play area. Some cottages have a sofa bed for extra versatility.
