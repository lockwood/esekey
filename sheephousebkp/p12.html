Southlands Cottage


Front view of Southlands Cottage


Southlands Cottage lounge

Our delightful terraced cottage offers both professionals and families the opportunity to relax and recharge.

Close to National Trust land, the River Thames, and a host of truly delightful cosy pubs and restaurants, and boutique style shops. The delightful walks along the Thames to the Bounty pub in Bourne End are within 5 minutes from your door.

Local amenity shops are to be found 1/2 mile away in Cookham Rise, where there is also a branch line railway station giving access to the main line train route to Paddington via Maidenhead.

The delightful towns of Marlow and Henley are only a few miles away.  Cookham Village is 3 miles from Maidenhead Town Centre.

Please note that there is no dedicated parking space at Southlands Cottage, but there are several free public car parks within a few minutes walk, and parking can mostly be found in the High Street or in the parking bays opposite the cottage.
