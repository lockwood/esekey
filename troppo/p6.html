  Welcome to Wisteria, Gardener's Bothy and The Smithy, our Dorney Self Catering Apartments providing quality accommodation in a unique location.

The apartments are set in the grounds of a Tudor house in a rural, peaceful location in the village of Dorney, yet only ten minutes' drive from Windsor.
Each of our apartments can accommodate singles, couples or a family, and when combined they can be block-booked by groups of up to 16 people (some on sofabeds).

 This is Tinker, one of our three Border Terriers. Their bark is much worse than their bite and they try to get in your way when you drive towards our gate. We welcome pets, but would appreciate notice of what and how many your intend to bring with you.

We attract regular visits from families (Legoland is a 15 minute drive away)(Dorney Court is a five minute drive).

The historic centres of Eton and Windsor, divided by the Thames, can be easily reached, either by a pleasant stroll along the Thames Path or by car in about ten minutes.

There is a large Sainsbury Superstore within 10 minutes' drive which is on your way if you come via the M4.
