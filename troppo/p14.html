Welcome to Wisteria, Gardener's Bothy and The Smithy, our Dorney Self Catering Apartments in Boveney and located close to Dorney Common,  Eton and Windsor. 

The apartments are set in the grounds of a Tudor house in a rural, peaceful location about a quarter of a mile from the village of Dorney. The River Thames and Boveney Lock are a short stroll away and Windsor and Eton are less than three miles from Dorney.  Maidenhead, Bray, Cookham, Bourne End and Henley are within half an hour's drive or less and Beaconsfield and Oxford are also within easy reach by car.  If you plan to visit London we recommend you take the train from Slough station to Paddington where the underground trains or buses will take you directly to the centre in less than an hour. You can park your car at Slough station.

We are also ideally situated for access to Legoland which is approximately a 20 minute drive away. After a busy time at Legoland there is plenty of space in the garden for children to enjoy.We do recommend a car for the duration of your stay.

Each of the apartments can accommodate singles, couples or a family, and when combined they can be block-booked by groups of up to 16 people (some on single 3ft sofabeds). We welcome wedding guests, hen parties, stag parties, Royal Ascot groups, family reunions and rowing groups, please ring Jan for more details 01753 827037

Dorney Lake which is to be the venue for the 2012 Rowing and Canoeing events is a two minute walk away. There is ample space there for walking or cycling in a quiet and safe environment.
