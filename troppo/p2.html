Welcome to Wisteria, Gardener's Bothy and The Smithy, our Dorney Self Catering Apartments in a unique location.
The apartments are set in the grounds of a Tudor house in a rural, peaceful location in the village of Dorney. The River Thames and Boveney Lock are a short stroll away and Windsor and Eton are less than three miles from Dorney. Maidenhead, Bray, Cookham, Bourne End and Henley are within half an hour's drive or less and Beaconsfield and Oxford are within easy reach by car. If you plan to visit London we suggest you take the train from Slough station to Paddington where the underground trains or buses will take you directly to the centre in less than an hour. This is the perfect place to stay if you plan a visit to Legoland. We recommend a car for the duration of your stay.

Each of our apartments can accommodate singles, couples or a family, and when combined they can be block-booked by groups of up to 16 people.

We attract regular visits from families (Legoland is a 15 minute drive away) (Dorney Court is a five minute drive).

The historic centres of Eton and Windsor, divided by the Thames, can be easily reached, either by a pleasant stroll along the Thames Path or by car in about fifteen minutes.

The Eton Rowing Lake (also Known as Dorney Rowing Lake) is 750 metres walk away, available for functions and the venue for many sports events including Human Race, Triathlons etc. It is also the venue for the 2012 Olympic Rowing and Canoeing events.

There is a large Sainsbury Superstore within 10 minutes' drive which is on your way if you come via the M4 Junction 7.
