Gardener's Bothy is a first floor barn conversion.

The Kitchen  is open plan and looks over the living area.  There is a gas hob, electric oven, fridge with small freezer box, microwave, kettle and toaster.

The Main Bedroom has a comfy king sized bed . There is also a single bed under the eaves which is ideal for children. The window overlooks the garden and if you are lucky you can just spot Windsor Castle to the East! This bedroom has a sloping ceiling
 

The second "bedroom" is 6ft x 6ft with a sloping ceiling and Velux window! It is not officially a bedroom, but there is a fitted 6ft long 3ft wide bed in it and we have been told more than once that it is extremely cosy!


The bathroom has a shower over the bath, with a shower screen, a WC and wash hand basin.

If all three of the single 3ft chair beds in the open plan living area are utilised, together with the master bedroom and the "tiny' bedroom" Gardener's Bothy can sleep 7 (a bit of a squeeze), which suits family reunions, or stag and hen parties. We can arrange for cutlery and crockery or dining furniture to be transferred from other apartments if necessary, depending on how many people are staying.
