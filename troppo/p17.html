The Smithy is a compact, but cosy twin-bedded open plan double aspect apartment on the ground floor, facing southeast. There are two sofa chairs, each opening out into 3ft wide single beds, so if there are four of you, it is possible to fit you all in, but it will be a bit of a squash, especially when the two sofa beds are pulled out at night.  Have a look at our Gallery and click on "Wisteria and The Smithy Floor Plan (6th from the top).  You can see the dimensions and layout. A curtain divides the bed area from the living area. There is a kitchenette with a small oven with a two ring hob and grill, a small fridge (no freezer compartment), toaster, kettle and microwave. The shower room has a shower cubicle, WC and wash hand basin. There is a table and chairs, cupboard and 26" flat screen digital TV with DVD player. In the summer there is space to sit outside and a charcoal barbecue with table and chairs if you enjoy outdoor dining.
