ACCESS STATEMENT

DORNEY SELF CATERING APARTMENTS MAY 2008

SELF CATERING APARTMENTS: GARDENERS BOTHY, WISTERIA and THE SMITHY

INTRODUCTION

This access statement describes our current facilities and services offered to our guests. This will enable potential guests to make an informed decision with regard to our flats and facilities. It also helps them decide about their particular needs.

It falls within the requirement of the Disability Discrimination Act (1995 revised 2005) (DDA). This access statement is a mandatory part of the quality assessment process for Visit Britain (Star Rating), but it also helps with specific access issues, decision making for potential guests, and legal obligations within the DDA (see above).

This access statement is placed on our website, is available within the information folder in each apartment and is available on demand from any of our staff.

It may be faxed to you, emailed or posted.

Dorney Self Catering Apartments comprises three separate self catering apartments, within one building, that was originally built as part of The Old Place, Boveney.

The building dates back to 1905, is red brick construction with tiled roof. Gardeners Bothy takes up the whole of the loft conversion on the first floor. The Smithy is to the rear of the building and Wisteria is to the front of the building. Wisteria is a conversion of the original coach house.

The Smithy and Wisteria are on the ground floor with no steps up to the entrance but with a threshold in each case.

Dorney Self Catering Apartments offers practical accommodation for families, who may use “sofa beds”. The star rating from Visit Britain is based on the accommodation of the number of bed places, which is of course, much less than the wider facility of sofa beds.

Facilities include:
  • Secure rural environment
  • Open garden areas for sitting out and playing games
  • Variety of livestock (geese, chickens etc)
  • Pets welcome
  • Clothes washing facilities and tumble dryer available free of charge
  • Tea, coffee etc provided for your arrival
  • Full kitchen facilities
  • Bath with shower in Gardeners Bothy
  • Shower rooms in Wisteria and The Smithy
  • Friendly helpful staff
PRE-ARRIVAL

Our booking process is mainly via the internet, with an email sent with directions and full information both on the website and our emails. Arrival is recommended by car, but guests can arrive by taxi. There are no immediate public transport facilities, with Taplow station being approximately 2 miles away, Slough being 4 miles away and the nearest bus stop is 2 miles away in Eton Wick.

There is ample parking for cars which is free.

We need notice of large vehicles such as mini buses, as these will be parked in a separate location from the main car park.

There are no tea rooms, farm shops or bars on the premises.

PUBLIC AREAS.

The gardens are shared between the three apartments and the lawn is not fenced off from the parking area. There is a pond that is fenced off, so parents should be aware of the whereabouts of their small children.

ENTERTAINMENT FACILITIES

There are large flat screen digital TVs in each apartment, with a DVD player. Jigsaw puzzles and games are available. BBQ and garden seating facilities are also available.

CONFERENCE AND MEETING ROOMS

There are no conference or meeting rooms.

ATTRACTIONS

There are no specific attractions. This is self catering accommodation for guests to enjoy the surroundings and explore the local tourist attractions.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

There is the legal requirement for no smoking within the buildings. There are areas outside with seating and ash trays for smokers.

ACCESS FOR THE DISABLED

Gardeners Bothy is on the first floor with narrow stairs, that are not shallow, and have a 90 degree spiral turn at the bottom.

Wisteria has a threshold. The access to the bathroom is limited due to the width of the door being less than 30 inches wide.

The Smithy has direct access with only a threshold, however, the shower room has an 8 inch step up to it.

The driveway is a gravel drive, although no problems have been experienced by wheelchair users.
