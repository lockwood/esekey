Wisteria is a ground floor apartment with one double bedroom with twin beds and a large mirrored fitted wardrobe. There is a living/dining room, open plan kitchen and bathroom. The apartment also has 3 sofa chairs that open out to form single 3ft wide beds. The bathroom has a shower cubicle, wash handbasin and WC  and the kitchen has all mod cons, including an electric cooker, dishwasher, microwave, fridge, toaster and kettle. The kitchen overlooks fields to the north west. There is a dining table and chairs and a 32" flat screen television with DVD player.  Wisteria is easily accessible, being on one level and it is possible to park very close to the entrance.
